# Procurement SOP

## Purchase Request
Departments must submit a purchase request form with business justification.
Requests above 20,000 CNY require department head approval.

## Vendor Selection
At least three vendor quotations are required for purchases above 10,000 CNY.
Vendor evaluation criteria include price, delivery lead time, and service quality.

## Contracting
Legal review is mandatory for all contracts above 50,000 CNY.
Contract signing authority follows the company authorization matrix.

## Payment Terms
Standard payment terms are Net 30 unless approved otherwise by finance.
Advance payments above 30% require CFO approval.
