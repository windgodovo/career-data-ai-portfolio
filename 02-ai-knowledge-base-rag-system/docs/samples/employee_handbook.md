# Employee Handbook

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are Monday to Friday, 09:00 to 18:00, with a 1-hour lunch break.
Flexible check-in is allowed between 08:30 and 10:00 if approved by the manager.

## Overtime Policy
Overtime must be pre-approved in writing by a direct manager.
For overtime on weekdays, compensatory leave can be requested within 60 days.

## Attendance
Employees must submit attendance corrections within 3 business days.
Repeated missing check-ins (3 or more times per month) will trigger an HR review.

## Performance Review
Formal performance reviews are conducted twice per year.
Review dimensions include delivery quality, collaboration, and ownership.
