# Employee Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
Employees receive 15 days annual leave per calendar year.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days into next year.

## Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days paid sick leave.
Medical certificate is required for absences longer than 2 days.

## Approval Rules
Leave request must be submitted at least 3 business days in advance.
Manager approval is required before travel bookings.
