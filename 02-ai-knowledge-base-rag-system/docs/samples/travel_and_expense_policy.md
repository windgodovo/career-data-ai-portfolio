# Travel and Expense Policy

## Travel Approval
Domestic travel requires manager approval at least 2 business days in advance.
International travel requires director approval at least 10 business days in advance.

## Booking Rules
Employees must use designated booking channels unless an exception is approved.
Economy class is required for flights shorter than 6 hours.

## Reimbursement Timeline
Expense reimbursement requests must be submitted within 15 calendar days after travel ends.
Finance processes compliant requests within 7 business days.

## Receipt Requirements
Valid VAT invoices are required for hotel and transportation reimbursement.
Meal expenses without receipts are capped at 50 CNY per meal.
